While the financial trading markets are undoubtedly the best place to make money through critical thinking and application of knowledge, nowhere else are people punished more severely for their lack of critical thinking.

Investing is risky, so make your decisions carefully!

As Howard Stanley Marks, who was ranked as the 374th richest person in the United States in 2017, once said.

"There are old investors and there are bold investors, but there are no old bold investors."

Don't think you can become a superhero just because you learned something new yesterday. Turning knowledge into meaningful action takes much more time than you think. Relax, take your time, and don't rush. You can lift very heavy things, but without proper training, you will hurt yourself.
